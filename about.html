About Us

Welcome to Pioneer Research Center Nepal (PRCN)

Established in 2019, PRCN is an independent research venture created with an aim to introduce positive chance by offering new insight through research. This institution attempts to explore innovative possibilities in various sectors including Tourism, Public policy, international relation, politics and other aspects of society. PRCN always looks forward for research partnership with international organizations, local bodies and government to help them achieve their objectives.

We stand distinct in the market because our limitation is not demarcated by the sector rather, we are interested in anything innovative, regardless of the sector they represent. PRCN provides innovative ideas, possibilities and implementation strategies based on open source, to policymakers, concerned stakeholders and interested groups.Àù

Our research has served over 14,325 People in 3 districts of Nepal
